School Library
was established in 1980 -

The St Xavier’s School Library is an exciting and dynamic learning space offering a large range of information resources for the entire school community. The Library provides a supportive, safe and interesting space for meeting, learning, reading and playing. The school library program is designed to develop and foster information literacy skills through the use of both print and online resources. Students are taught to research in an efficient, effective and safe manner, equipping them with vital skills for the future. Students visit the Library regularly to borrow and read books, play games and research using technology such as World Book Online. The students are also now able to borrow and read e-books through our Oliver website. Looking for more information regarding the St Xavier’s School Library? Simply follow the link to our Oliver Library website for all the latest news and information from the Library.

what's new

  • School canteen is new!


    This project is the refurbishment of the 22 year old school cafeteria at St. Xavier’s School. As the school evolves, the cafeteria was no longer able to handle the current student body and activities. The design team and the school directors envisioned an environment that promotes a culture of good interaction and well-being amongst students. Already at its maximum capacity, the previously congested cafeteria is now considerably de-cluttered and open. The palette of concrete, steel, and wood finishes characterize a “raw” ambiance that relates to the same rawness described by the salubriousness of organic food.
